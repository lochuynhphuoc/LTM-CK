Today, AI algorithms power everything from personalized recommendations on streaming platforms to sophisticated voice assistants like Siri and Alexa. The emergence of Generative AI has further revolutionized the landscape, enabling machines to create complex text, realistic images, and even computer code with minimal human intervention.
However, this rapid advancement brings significant challenges. Issues surrounding data privacy, algorithmic bias, and the potential displacement of jobs are sparking intense global debates.
